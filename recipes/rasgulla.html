Rasgulla

Description

Rasgulla, also known as Rosogolla, Rosgola, or Rasagola is a South Asian syrupy dessert popular in the Indian subcontinent and regions with South Asian diaspora. It is made from ball-shaped dumplings of chhena (an Indian cottage cheese) and semolina dough, cooked in light sugar syrup made of sugar.This is done until the syrup permeates the dumplings.
