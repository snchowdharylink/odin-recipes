Hyderabadi Dum Biryani

Description

Hyderabadi biryani, also known as Hyderabadi dum biryani, is a style of biryani from Hyderabad, India made with basmati rice and goat or chicken meat and cooked with the dum pukht method.

Originating in the kitchens of the Nizam of Hyderabad, it combines elements of Hyderabadi and Mughlai cuisines. Hyderabad biryani is a key dish in Hyderabadi cuisine.

  • 1 cup plain yogurt

  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons chile powder
  • 1 teaspoon biryani masala powder (such as Dunya®)
